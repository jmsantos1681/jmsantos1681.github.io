2016 ACM-ICPC Asia-Manila Regionals
Branding: Contest event

The ACM-ICPC, considered as the “olympics of programming competitions,” is the oldest, largest and the most prestigious programming contest in the world. Last 2016, the Ateneo de Manila University hosted this Regional Contest

Main graphic came from the basic shapes of some PH icons/figures: Flag, Fauna, Mangoes, Halo-Halo, foods on a stick, LRT, a lot of buildings, pandesal, etc.
