2018 International Conference on Computers in Education
Branding: Conference

ICCE is an annual conference organized by the Asia-Pacific Society for Computers in Education (APSCE). On its 26th year, it's the Philippines, through Ateneo de Manila University (ADMU) and the Ateneo Laboratory for the Learning Sciences (ALLS), who hosted this conference.

Main graphic came from Alexis Andres, who utilized the three main colors of the country's flag (slighty modified so it'll still jell with one another in the logo), and a weaving pattern from one of the Philippines' indigenous people. I took care of how this graphic will be utilized across all the materials needed.
