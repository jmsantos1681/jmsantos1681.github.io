The 3rd APSCE International Conference on Future Language Learning
Branding: Conference

The 3rd APSCE International Conference on Future Language Learning (ICFULL) organized by the Ateneo de Manila University and the Asia-Pacific Society for Computers in Education (APSCE) provides a platform for academics and researchers from transdisciplinary areas, including cognitive neural scientists, linguistic researchers, pedagogical designers, theorists of language learning as well as practitioners in various language learning arenas in the world to share and exchange ideas to strengthen research, theories and practice, and explore possibilities for advancing future language learning.

Main graphics are a play of the basic shapes, to help with the thought of language being the base for most of the topics in the conference. Main colors derived from the country's main colors.
